Digital Gadget

A digital gadget is a small electronic device that uses technology to perform specific tasks. Examples include smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, drones and cameras. These devices often have screens, connect to the internet, and run apps or software to help with things like communication, entertainment, photography or work. They are designed to be portable, user-friendly, and make daily life easier or more fun


Drone

Drones are flying machines controlled remotely or by computers. They can take photos, videos, or carry things. People use drones for fun, work, or even deliveries. They are easy to fly and come in many sizes.

Basic Componets in Drone

1.Frame: The body or structure that holds everything together, like a skeleton.

2.Motors: Spin the propellers to make the drone fly.

3.Battery: Powers the drone, like its energy source

4.Camera (optional): Takes photos or videos, if included.
